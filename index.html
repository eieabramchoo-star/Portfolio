Abram was a student of St Andrew's School (Secondary) from 2022 to 2026. A well-mannered and conscientious student, Abram was a pleasant addition to the class. He possessed a lively disposition and was well liked by his classmates. He consistently demonstrated enthusiasm and a positive learning attitude towards his studies. As a motivated learner, he was selected to participate in the Applied Learning Module at the Institute of Technical Education (ApLM@ITE) in 2025. He earned the Silver Award in the National Youth Achievement Award (NYAA) Programme in 2025. Through the NYAA Programme, Abram developed self-reliance, perseverance, and a strong sense of responsibility.

For his Co-Curricular Activity, Abram was a dedicated member of the Saints Choir. Given his unwavering commitment, he was made Section Leader in 2024, and he continued to guide his peers with vision and enthusiasm. Together with his peers, he played a significant role in helping the Choir to attain a Distinction in the Singapore Youth Festival Arts Presentation in 2023. Given his flair for singing, Abram performed in various performances: the Asia Kodaly Symposium 2022, Cantabile 2023 with the Methodist Girls' School, and the Combined Performing Arts Concert: Vivace, Keep the Flame Burning Brightly in 2024. Abram contributed to community outreach efforts by performing at Kwong Wai Shiu Hospital during Chinese New Year and the Prefects' Commendation in 2024. Through his involvement in the Saints Choir, Abram demonstrated leadership and showed profound dedication to the arts community.

With a passion to serve the community, Abram participated in many Values in Action (VIA) activities such as the Green Carnival held in school in 2022 where, together with his peers, he set up a booth promoting recycling efforts in the school. In 2023, he spent time interacting and engaging the elderly from Calvary Community Care through games. In 2024, Abram participated in a litter-picking activity at Potong Pasir, demonstrating his care for the environment. To encourage civic responsibility, in 2025, Abram and his classmates set up a booth in school during recess to encourage students to make pledges not to litter and to exercise greater responsibility for their environment. Through participation in these projects, he displayed his personal commitment to being a concerned and responsible citizen. In 2026, Abram and his classmates contributed to the school's sustainability efforts by planting herbs and edible plants in the school aquaponics plot and Therapeutic Garden, promoting environmental stewardship and food sustainability.

Abram is a highly motivated individual whose determination and contribution in the arts community will undoubtedly lead him to success in his future pursuits.
